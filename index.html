Driven: Europe's Premier Club for Formula One Fans

Welcome!

Driven is the premier club for Formula One fans in Europe, offering a range of premium experiences, advance and limited event ticket purchase programs, local events in cities across Europe and an annual catalog of memorable race day experiences. In addition the club affords members exclusive access to drivers, teams and crew areas of the most popular F1 circuts in the world. We have been in operation since the late 1970s and utilize this wealth of expertise to provide our members with top tier service.

Exciting benefits of our club include:

Ticket Pre-Purchase Program

Members of Driven are given access to ticket offers for races throughout the season before public ticket sales commence. This allows our members to gain access to advance tickets for in-demand races, often at much better prices than those offered upon public sale.

Exclusive Driver Meet & Greets

Through our long-standing partnership with Formula One and relationships with the top teams in the world, Driven offers ticketed private events with the most sought-after drivers in the world! These exciting events give our members access not found anywhere else in the Formula One community.

Club Events Across Europe

With six premier club locations across Europe, Driven members are able to connect with the local Formula One community across the continent. We also offer reciprocity with clubs in the Americas and Asia Pacific, allowing you to stay close to the action no matter where you travel.
